# EX-02-Cross-Platform-Prompting-Evaluating-Diverse-Techniques-in-AI-Powered-Text-Summarization

## AIM
To evaluate and compare the effectiveness of prompting techniques across multiple AI platforms for summarizing a technical article on **“The Basics of Blockchain Technology.”**

Experiment: Test and compare how different pattern models respond to various prompts (broad or unstructured) versus basic prompts (clearer and more refined) across multiple scenarios. Analyze the quality, accuracy, and depth of the generated responses.

---

# ALGORITHM

Here’s a well-structured professional workflow used to generate the comparative report for the experiment:

---

## Step 1: Select the Topic
Choose a technical article titled:

### “The Basics of Blockchain Technology”

The article should contain approximately 500 words explaining:
- Definition of blockchain
- Working principle
- Cryptography and decentralization
- Real-world applications
- Advantages of blockchain technology

---

## Step 2: Select AI Platforms
Choose multiple AI tools for comparison:

1. ChatGPT  
2. Gemini  
3. Meta AI  
4. Claude  
5. Cohere Command  

---

## Step 3: Prepare the Prompt
Use a common summarization prompt for all AI platforms.

### Prompt Used:
> “Summarize the article ‘The Basics of Blockchain Technology’ in simple words suitable for undergraduate students.”

---

## Step 4: Generate Summaries
Provide the same blockchain article and prompt to all selected AI models.

Collect:
- ChatGPT summary
- Gemini summary
- Meta AI summary
- Claude summary
- Cohere Command summary

---

## Step 5: Compare the Outputs
Analyze each summary based on the following parameters:

- Accuracy
- Clarity
- Simplicity
- Technical depth
- User engagement
- Readability for students
- Real-world examples used

---

## Step 6: Create Comparison Table
Prepare comparison tables showing:
- Strengths of each AI model
- Weaknesses of each AI model
- Overall quality of summaries

---

## Step 7: Rank the AI Models
Rank the models according to undergraduate-level understanding and summarization quality.

### Final Ranking:
1. Claude  
2. Gemini  
3. ChatGPT  
4. Meta AI  
5. Cohere Command  

---

## Step 8: Draw Conclusion
Analyze which AI model provides:
- The most beginner-friendly explanation
- The most academic explanation
- The most engaging explanation
- The most technically detailed explanation

Conclude the experiment based on overall performance and educational usefulness.
